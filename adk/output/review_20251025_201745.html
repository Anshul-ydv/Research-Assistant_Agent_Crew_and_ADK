2. Photometry and preliminary modelling of Type IIb Supernova 2017gpn (2018)

Authors: Maria Pruzhinskaya, Elena Balakina, Alexander Moskvitin, Sergey Blinnikov

Abstract summary: MASTER OT J033744.97+723159.0/SN 2017gpn was discovered in the error-box of LIGO/Virgo G299232 gravitational wave alert. The spectrum of SN 2017gpn is consistent with a Type IIb supernova. In this work we present the photometry of 20 epochs of observations performed with CCD-photometer on the Zeiss-1000 telescope. The light curves in B and Rc filters were obtained. The multicolour light curves were also modelled numerically using the one-dimensional radiation hydro-dynamical code STELLA.
